deldup

12 novembre 2003

What is deldup?

deldup.pl deletes duplicates from a UNIX mailbox, relying on a checksum of the body. It modifies the mbox in-place, so make a backup before applying and use at your own risk.

deldup.pl is released under the GNU GPL.

Usage

First make sure you have Perl with String::CRC32 and Mail::Box modules installed (under Debian the packages are libstring-crc32-perl and libmail- box-perl), then simply run:

deldup.pl filename
